- MAUIControls
  > **Demo of the main MAUI controls including:**

     - Radio
     - Checkboxes 
	 - Switch
	 - ImageButton
   - Text Input
      - Editor
	  - Entry

		- VerticalStackLayout

	- AbsoluteLayout

	- ContentView

	- CollectionView
	- IndicatorView
	- ListView
	- Picker
	- TableView

	- MVVM Basics
	- MVVM Library (Microsoft Toolkit)
	- MVVM Generated
- MauiStyles
   > **Customizing the look and feel of your controls and data.**
- MauiGraphics
   > **Displaying and creating graphics**
   	- Basics
	- Dynamic graphics
	- Timer Clock
	- Generating Barcodes (ZXing Library)
	- Reading Barcodes
- MauiNavigation
   > **Navigation and passing of parameters across views**
- MauiDevices
   > **Customizing the look and feel of your controls and data.**
- MauiMedia
   > **Camera and barcode reading and misc**
